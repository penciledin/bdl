if&when

if&when is a literary magazine that aims to not only present creativity, but foster it. To that end, issues include short fiction, short creative nonfiction, poetry and single literary lines, as well as activities and prompts to encourage picking up a pen and writing.

Co-founded by Victoria Billings and Benjamin Lawless. Designed by Benjamin Lawless. Published by Penciled In.
